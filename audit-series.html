SAP & Withdrawal Patterns

Satisfactory Academic Progress is a Title IV requirement — and the data to monitor it is already in your SIS. GPA below 2.0, completion pace below 67%, exceeding 150% maximum time frame. Then: the withdrawal patterns that signal deeper problems — multiple W's in a term, complete term withdrawals that trigger R2T4 refund calculations, and serial withdrawers across 3+ terms.
